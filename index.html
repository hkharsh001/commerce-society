About the Department

The Department of Commerce at NSCBM Govt. College Hamirpur offers rigorous undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to develop financial insight, business ethics, and real-world skills. We organise workshops, industrial visits, seminars and student-led projects to bridge theory and practice.
